Detailed Description

template<typename FloatType>
class ame::Slide< FloatType >

Smooth values logarithmically.

y (n) = y (n-1) + ((x (n) - y (n-1))/slide)

Note
SlideUp and SlideDown are affected by the update interval of the process.
